## The Brief

Avoidant/Restrictive Food Intake Disorder (ARFID) affects individuals who struggle with limited food variety and eating challenges. Traditional recovery methods often lack accessible digital tools to support the food chaining technique—a gradual exposure therapy approach.

**The Challenge:**
- Limited digital tools for ARFID recovery support
- Need for accessible, mobile-first solution
- Tracking progress through food chaining methodology
- Supporting users through gradual exposure therapy

## The Strategy

Develop a progressive web application that guides users through the food chaining process, tracks their progress, and provides encouragement throughout their recovery journey. The focus was on accessibility, simplicity, and evidence-based methodology.

**Why This Approach:**
Progressive web apps provide native-app-like experiences without installation barriers. For users managing eating disorders, reducing friction to access support tools is critical. The food chaining methodology is evidence-based and proven effective for ARFID recovery.

## The Execution

**Food Chaining Methodology**
Implemented a structured approach to food chaining, allowing users to identify safe foods and gradually introduce similar foods through small, manageable steps.

**Progress Tracking**
Built comprehensive tracking features to monitor food introductions, success rates, and patterns over time. Visual progress indicators provide motivation and insight.

**Mobile-First Design**
Created an intuitive, accessible interface optimized for mobile devices. Users can quickly log meals and track progress wherever they are.

**Privacy-Focused Architecture**
Designed with privacy as a priority, ensuring sensitive health data remains secure and under user control.

## The Result

ARFID Chainer provides accessible, evidence-based support for individuals managing eating disorders through digital tools.

**Impact:**
- Accessible recovery support tool for ARFID community
- Evidence-based food chaining methodology implementation
- Privacy-focused health data management
- Mobile-first experience for on-the-go support
